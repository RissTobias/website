Start by curiosity. Flourish by indulgence.





This is my philosophy. This is what drives me.




From intricate biological systems to precise engineered machines, complex things always fascinated me. That's what brought me into medical engineering, an interdisciplinary field where I could have real impact. But even the best ideas are worthless if they never reach the market.



Business understanding and an economic mindset became equally important. I sharpened my skills in consulting and venture capital positions. But my real passion lies in entrepreneurship, strategizing and building things from the ground up.



I build for the sake of building. Software, robotics, side projects. I also co-founded AIM Innovate, a program that guides students from various backgrounds through the process of taking a raw idea to a certified medical product.




Right now, my curiosity pulls in two directions. AI with real impact in medicine, especially foundation models and the data layer beneath them. And Generative Engine Optimization, helping brands show up in AI answers, not just search results.
